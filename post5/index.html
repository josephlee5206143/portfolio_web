What I did
I helped greenvines come up with a new vision: say goodbye to hallo. We learned from FB delivery data that greenvines' main customer base regards products as family members. By saying goodbye to old products and brands, users can accept the new brand image and stimulate purchases.
我幫助 greenvines 定訂新的視覺方案“好好說再見”。我們透過FB投放數據與定性訪談得知，greenvines 的主要客群將產品視為家人成員。透過對舊的產品、品牌說再見，讓用戶接納新的品牌形象，刺激購買。
